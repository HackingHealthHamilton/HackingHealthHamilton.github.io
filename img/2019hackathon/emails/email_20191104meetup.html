Newsletter: Value stream mapping with Steve Periera at Oct.24 Meetup
 

In the lead up to the Hackathon, we invited the community to attend a meetup on October 24, featuring Steve Periera. A transformation expert/ business tech coach, Pereira took participants through a value stream exercise.

Participants came from far and wide, including Hamilton, Oakville, Mississauga, Burlington and Waterloo, These skills can be applied to ongoing projects or new ones to pitch at our November Hackathon. He provided insights on how to transform your business ideas.
